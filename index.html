How to Play:

Build a sandcastle to protect an area from incoming waves.

Click on the beach to place sand blocks. Build walls higher by placing blocks on top of existing ones.

The closer to the water, the more points you earn, but the higher risk of wave damage.

Survive 10 waves within 10 minutes to win!
